Case Study · 01

Teaching Admin Systematised

Self-Managed · 3 teaching roles + 2 private classes · 8+ years

Problem

Juggling 3 teaching roles and 2 private tutoring classes with no system made it hard to track schedules, prepare materials on time, and keep student records organised.

Solution

Built a personal system using Google Calendar for scheduling, Google Sheets to track student progress, and Google Drive to organise all lesson materials and records by class and date.

6+
